Tell me about your active listening approach in customer service.
I always understand the customer clearly before responding. I take steps to avoid misunderstandings by repeating key points. This helps build trust with customers over time. I respond appropriately based on what they actually need.
How do you ensure clear communication?
I use clear language that anyone can understand. I avoid jargon and complex terminology. I make it a point to be polite in all interactions. I always explain the next steps before ending any conversation.
Let's talk about handling complaints.
When a customer complains, I stay calm and composed. I listen carefully without interrupting them. I acknowledge the issue and show I understand their frustration. Then I look for a solution together with the customer.
What makes a solution effective?
An effective solution addresses the issue the customer raised. It needs to be realistic within our capabilities. It must be explained clearly so there is no confusion. And it should be delivered in a timely way.
How would you describe your professional attitude?
I maintain a respectful attitude with everyone. Punctuality is a core value for me. I believe in polite communication at all times. I take full responsibility for my actions and results.
How do you handle working under pressure?
I stay calm and focused when under pressure. I prioritize tasks to manage my time efficiently. I communicate clearly with colleagues and managers. I focus on solutions instead of getting stressed about problems.
